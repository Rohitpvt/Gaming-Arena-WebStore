Welcome to Feudal Japan

Live the intertwined stories of Naoe, an adept shinobi assassin from Iga Province, and Yasuke, the legendary African samurai. Against the backdrop of the turbulent late Sengoku period, this duo must fight for their own destinies and usher in a new era for Japan.

A New Creed Rises

Straddling stealth and might, Shadows lets you slip through the darkness as Naoe or stand resolute as Yasuke. The game features non-linear missions, environmental interactivity, and dynamic lighting that evolves with time and seasons.

Dual Protagonist Gameplay

Play as Naoe, agile and stealthy, trained in ninjutsu and shadow techniques, or Yasuke, a fearsome warrior with raw strength and samurai combat skills. Switch between them to exploit their unique strengths.
